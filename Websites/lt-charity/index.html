About us

In 2015, Zakira Uzma Taqvi and a Group pf Youths along with the supervision and Guidance of Imam - E - Zamana ajf, initiated the unending Journey which is Famously known as, BAIT-E-ZEHRA. Keeping in mind the one and only motive of this organization is to reach out to those who are underprivileged. It made sure to prevail equality amongst the community and connecting hearts with all sorts of help according to the islamic teachings and moral ethics of a democratic society

Chair Person: Scholor Uzma Zehra Taqvi
